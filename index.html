Late fee: Please be courteous and ensure that if you are receiving service to please arrive on time for your scheduled appointment. There is a 6 minute grace period. After 7th minutes, your appointment will be cancelled or rescheduled If you are going to be late, please notify me by phone as soon as possible. Please note that even if you call or text notifying me that you're going to be late or you're having issues finding my location this policy still applies. There is a late charge of $15 that will be applied to your total service amount. Meaning at the 7th minutes of being late, you will automatically be charged a $15 inconvenience fee in addition to your remaining balance. If you are more than 7 minutes late then your appointment will be cancelled (at my discretion) and you will be charged a cancellation fee. I strive and make every effort to ensure the timeline for client services is maintained. This policy seeks to protect all those who are booked for appointments.

Confirmation of appointments: If you do not confirm your appointment the day before your appointment date I will assume you are not coming and you will be charged 100% of your service fee.

Rescheduling: if you have to reschedule your appointment please do so 24 hours in advance same day rescheduling will result in a 50% fee even if it’s for the same day at a different hour.

Cancellation: You have up to 24 hours to cancel your appointment this can be done by logging into my appointment site. If you don’t cancel 24 hours in advance you will be charged the full amount of your service.

COVID Guidelines: Face Mask is required for me to service you if you don’t have one you can purchase one for $2.00. If you are feeling ill or have been exposed to COVID PLEASE RESCHEDULE.

SPECIAL NOTES: Please ensure that your hair is the length of a rice grain or 1/4th of an inch.
